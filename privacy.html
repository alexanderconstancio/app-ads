If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hey@alexbuilds.dev.

1. Information We Collect

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the game. Google AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers to provide and improve advertising services. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information about how they handle user data.

We do not use any analytics tools or other third-party SDKs.

3. Advertising and Tracking

Solo Jumper integrates Google AdMob according to their official documentation. AdMob may serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your device information. Cookies or similar technologies may be used by Google AdMob to personalize advertising content.

You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and your choices by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

4. Children's Privacy

Solo Jumper is accessible to children under 13 and does not contain explicit content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to support internal operations, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parents or guardians who believe that their child has provided personal information can contact us at hey@alexbuilds.dev to request deletion.

5. User Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal information we have collected. If you would like to request deletion, please contact us at hey@alexbuilds.dev.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at hey@alexbuilds.dev.

Please review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
